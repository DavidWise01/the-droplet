What It Is

A small (~3.5 m) teardrop Trisolaran probe whose hull is made of 'strong-interaction-force material' — giving it a perfectly mirror-smooth, near-absolute-zero, indestructible surface. In the Doomsday Battle it rams straight through Earth's combined space fleet, destroying roughly two thousand warships in minutes.

Across The Three Media

📖 BOOKSNETFLIX ’24

The Droplet's annihilation of the fleet is a book-two set-piece; Netflix brings it forward into season one. (Tencent's 2023 series adapts book one only, so its full battle is still to come there.)

The Real Science [FLUFF]

FLUFF. The strong nuclear force acts only at femtometre range — you cannot build a macroscopic hull out of it. A flawless, frictionless, indestructible mirror is fantasy. The honest nod: ultra-dense degenerate matter (white-dwarf / neutron-star material) is real, and unimaginably strong — but it is not a teardrop you can fly through a fleet.
