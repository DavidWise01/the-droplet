# The Droplet (水滴) · DRP

> a perfect mirror that kills — the strong-force probe

A single-technology dissection from the Three-Body universe — part of [The Technologia of Trisolaris](https://davidwise01.github.io/trisolaris-tech/), a companion to the [Three-Body Problem pocket universe](https://davidwise01.github.io/three-body-problem/).

**Media:** 📖 BOOKS, NETFLIX ’24  
**Real-science verdict:** FLUFF

**What it is.** A small (~3.5 m) teardrop Trisolaran probe whose hull is made of 'strong-interaction-force material' — giving it a perfectly mirror-smooth, near-absolute-zero, indestructible surface. In the Doomsday Battle it rams straight through Earth's combined space fleet, destroying roughly two thousand warships in minutes.

**Across the three media.** The Droplet's annihilation of the fleet is a book-two set-piece; Netflix brings it forward into season one. (Tencent's 2023 series adapts book one only, so its full battle is still to come there.)

**The science.** FLUFF. The strong nuclear force acts only at femtometre range — you cannot build a macroscopic hull out of it. A flawless, frictionless, indestructible mirror is fantasy. The honest nod: ultra-dense degenerate matter (white-dwarf / neutron-star material) is real, and unimaginably strong — but it is not a teardrop you can fly through a fleet.
